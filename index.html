About

I am a Professor at the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Ningbo University. My research centers on optimization theory and methods, with a particular focus on splitting algorithms, proximal methods, and their applications in signal processing, image processing, and tensor computations.

Research Interests

Optimization Theory and Methods Splitting Algorithms Proximal Methods ADMM Primal-Dual Algorithms DC Programming Tensor Computation Image Processing Signal Processing Sparse Optimization
